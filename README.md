# Retail Sales EDA

## 📌 Objective
Analyze retail sales data to uncover patterns and business insights.

## Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib

## Analysis Performed
- Sales by Product Category
- Sales by Gender
- Sales by Age Group
- Monthly Sales Trend

## Key Insights
- Electronics category generates the highest revenue
- Female customers spend more than male customers
- Age group 45–60 contributes the most to sales
- Month 5 has the highest sales

## Dataset
Retail Sales dataset from Kaggle

## Outcome
Identified key sales patterns and customer behavior trends
